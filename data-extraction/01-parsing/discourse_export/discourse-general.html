TOPIC 2 - MozItalians ( Daniele)

  • MozItalians is a team inside the Mozilla Italia community with the focus about the program like Campus or Tech Speaker, events and recruiting that act as bridge with the rest of the community.
  • There is the MozItalians council that actually is composed by member that are active in their volunteering and that decide for the rest of the Mozitalians members.
  • For every programs inside they are independent in their activities but they can collaborate with the others to join the forces and improve the community itself.
  • For us is our “Participation Team” in fact is mainly task is do participation stuff.
  • QUESTIONS:
  • How many Reps do you have ? and active Mozillians ? FSA ?
  • Core community is about 10 people
  • Do you have a wiki page or page where someone can find your community?
  • wiki page coming soon

TOPIC 3 - Maker Party Stunt & SUMO Support (Sierra)

  • To build awareness for Maker Party and for our efforts in the EU with copyright reform, we are planning a one-day global contribute-a-thon on October 5th to the Creative Commons public domain, where we will make history by contributing the most images to the commons in one day and by the most countries at once. Yes, it’s ambitious but we know we can do it!! This means we need 20k photos submit We’ll have specific instructions coming soon but for now, start collecting all your best photos. We will have a few challenges (with prizes) among the Social Champions to see which communities can submit the most! So if you’re not a part of the Social Champions - it’s time to join! E-mail me at sreed@mozilla.com, Telegram: sierrar, Twitter: @simplxesierra IRC: sreed
  • Today is also a SUMO support day to go with the recent release of Firefox 49. Myself, along with other Mozilla Staff and SUMO Social Supporters are hanging out in the #SUMO room on IRC. If you’re part of the SUMO Social Support crew and have access to Sprinklr, let’s have some fun answering questions and letting everyone know how great this recent release is. For questions on how to get more involved, contact me!
  • QUESTIONS:
  • Will there be any consideration for non-mainstream social networks (Diaspora, etc.)?
  • Good question - right now, we are focused on having everyone contribute to the Creative Commons public domain online.
  • A wiki page about that?
  • I’ll bring this idea up to Elizabeth Hull and Maura Touhy. I’ll try to have an answer by the next reps call and have something as a public page for information on how to get involved.
